Equal opportunity

Request interview adjustments

You can ask for interview adjustments if you have a physical or mental condition. You can also ask for adjustments if you have a neurodiversity.

Adjustments could include:

  • level, step-free access to the interview room
  • having the interview at a certain time of day
  • communication support, like a British Sign Language interpreter

We’ll show your answer on your application, so the company can make adjustments for your interview.

If you don’t want to mention a health condition in your application, you can wait and ask for adjustments later. Discuss your needs when the company offers you an interview.
