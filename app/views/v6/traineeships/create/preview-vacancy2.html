Traineeship details

A Traineeship is an education and training programme with work experience that is focused on giving young people the skills that employers are looking for. At its core are work preparation training, English and maths for those who need it, and a high-quality work experience placement.

Traineeships help unlock your great potential and give you the work preparation training, maths and English and work experience needed to get an Apprenticeship or other job.

Are you eligible?

  • Are you currently not in a job? Do you have little or no work experience?
  • Are you aged 16 to 24 and qualified below Level 4?

During your 2 weeks of work experience as a Customer Relations Trainee you will:

  • Identify potential wholesale stockists
  • Contact potential stockists to introduce Rockit Kids and find out if they would be interested in placing a wholesale order (semi-scripted)
  • Record communications and progress using a spreadsheet

What work experience and training will the trainee get?

This 10-week Traineeship opportunity includes 8 weeks of work preparation training and 2 weeks of work experience.

  • Real Work Placement of minimum 70 hours
  • You could get a Level 1 Award qualification in Work Skills Development and a Level 1 Award in Customer Service
  • Help with English and maths functional skills, if needed and digital support
  • Ongoing support to access employment, apprenticeship, education, or training opportunities

The Traineeship is unpaid, but you can still claim out of work benefits.
