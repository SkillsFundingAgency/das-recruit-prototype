Disability Confidence

This apprenticeship is part of the government’s Disability Confidence scheme.

This employer will normally offer you an interview if you:

  • say that you have a disability or health condition
  • meet the minimum requirements for this apprenticeship

There might be some situations when the company can’t meet this commitment. For example, if an apprenticeship is particularly popular, the employer may need to limit the amount of people they interview.

Do you want to say that you have a disability or health condition?

There is no legal requirement to disclose a disability or health condition when applying for an apprenticeship.
