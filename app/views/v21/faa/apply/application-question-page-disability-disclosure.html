Equal opportunity

Disability Confident

King Recruitment has signed up to the government’s Disability Confidence scheme. They want to help people with a disability get an interview for this apprenticeship.

The company will try to offer an interview if you:

  • have a disability
  • meet the essential requirements for this apprenticeship
Understanding disability

Almost 1 in 4 working age adults in the UK have a disability. A disability is a long-term physical or mental condition that affects your daily life.

Read how the Equality Act 2010 describes disability (opens in new tab)

If this advert gets a lot of applications, the company may not be able to offer everybody with a disability an interview. They’ll still interview a fair proportion of applicants with a disability.

If you opt in to Disability Confident, you don’t need to provide any information about your condition. If you need support at an interview, you can ask for this in the Request interview adjustments of your application.
