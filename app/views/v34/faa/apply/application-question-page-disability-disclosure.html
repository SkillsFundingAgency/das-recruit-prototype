Equal opportunity

Disability Confident scheme

If you have disability of long-term health condition, this scheme could help you get an interview.

King Recruitment wants to help more disabled people reach the interview stage for this apprenticeship.

They’ll offer a fair proportion of interviews to people with a disability or long-term health condition.

Using the Disability Confident scheme

You can apply under this scheme if you have a disability or long-term health condition. This includes non-visible disabilities and conditions.

If you're unsure about your condition, check the definition of disability under the Equality Act 2010 (opens in new tab).

To be considered for an interview, you’ll still need to meet the essential requirements for this apprenticeship.

You don’t need to tell us any details about your condition to apply under this scheme.
