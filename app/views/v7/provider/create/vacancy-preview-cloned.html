What will the apprentice be doing?

With these structured training programmes you’ll divide your time between the classroom and electronics or mechanical engineering workshop. Our schemes follow Institute for Apprenticeships’ Level 3 standards, with bespoke modules added to prepare you for a career in Defence. Start your apprenticeship with a year at college, then gradually move onto work placements on real projects in our laboratories and workshops.

Change

What training will the apprentice take and what qualification will the apprentice get at the end?

You will spend your first year full time at college, developing theoretical and practical skills whilst studying for BTEC Diploma in Engineering at an appropriate level to your prior experience. More details can be found on the Civil Service Jobs website (below).

Following this, you will follow a structured work placement program, working on real projects in our groups.
