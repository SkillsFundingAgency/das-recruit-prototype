Your employment

Who you’ll work for

King Recruitment

What you’ll do

You’ll be at the heart of keeping our busy office running smoothly:

  • Be part of King Recruitment’s commitment to being an inclusive, open and supportive workplace
  • Keep our databases and customer relationship management software (CRM) accurate and up-to-date, following best GDPR practice
  • Assist members of the team to create reports that track company performance
  • Take minutes of company meetings and transcribe and circulate them accurately and quickly
  • Talk with clients on the phone and help schedule appointments for them to meet our recruiters
  • Printing, photocopying, scanning, typing up minutes, general office support work including some receptionist duties and handling post

About this company

King Recruitment is a leading recruitment company specialising in medical, science and manufacturing recruitment. Our mission is to find people jobs that help them meet and exceed their potential. We’ve helped over 1,000 people into work. We have a staff of 15 employees and our office is busy.

www.kingrecruitment.com

Your training

Who you’ll train with

Sheffield Professional College

What you’ll learn

You will be taught the Business Administrator Apprenticeship Standard LEVEL 3.

Attendance: 1 day per month alternating between Microsoft teams and in-person at Sheffield Professional College, Jam Avenue Campus.

You’ll be assessed with:

  • Knowledge Test
  • Portfolio based interview
  • Interview at the end of your course

Apprenticeship standard you’ll achieve

Business administrator (Level 3)

Level 3 is equivalent to A-levels
