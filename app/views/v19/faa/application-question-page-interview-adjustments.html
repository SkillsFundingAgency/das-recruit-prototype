Equality

Request interview adjustments

If you have a disability or health condition, you can ask for interview adjustments so you’re not disadvantaged.

Adjustments include:

  • clevel, step-free access to the interview room
  • a quiet environment
  • communication support, like a British Sign Language interpreter

We’ll show your answer on your application, so the employer or training provider can make the adjustments for your interview.

Do you want to request any interview adjustments?

You don’t need to request adjustments at this point. You can talk to the employer directly if they offer you an interview.
