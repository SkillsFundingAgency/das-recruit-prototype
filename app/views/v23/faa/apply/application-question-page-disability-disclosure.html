Equal opportunity

Disability Confident scheme

This company encourages disabled people to apply for this apprenticeship.

They’ve joined the government’s Disability Confident scheme. This means a fair proportion of interviews for this apprenticeship will be offered to disabled applicants.

You can apply under the scheme if you:

  • have a physical or hidden disability, or long-term health condition
  • Meet the essential requirements for this apprenticeship

You’ll then be considered for a Disability Confident interview.

You don’t need to tell us any further details about your condition to apply under this scheme.
