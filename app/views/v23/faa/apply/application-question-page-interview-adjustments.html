Interview adjustments

Ask for support at an interview

You can ask for support at an interview if you have a disability, health condition or neurodiversity.

You can ask for your interview to have reasonable adjustments. Adjustments could include things like:

  • step-free access to the interview room
  • a quiet place for the interview

It’s your choice when to discuss your needs

If you make a request here, we’ll show it on your application.

You can choose to select No on this question, even if you need adjustments. You can wait and see if the company offers you an interview first, and then discuss your needs with them.
